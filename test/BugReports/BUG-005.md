# Bug Report: BUG-005

**Title**: Copilot drawer does not close when clicking the ESC key
**Environment**: MacOS, Safari, Desktop
**Module**: AI Copilot (AI)

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Log into the Dashboard.
2. Click the Copilot button to open the AI drawer.
3. Press the `Escape` (ESC) key on the keyboard.

## Expected Result
The drawer should close, providing standard accessibility and UX behavior for overlay components.

## Actual Result
Nothing happens. The user must manually click the 'X' button or click outside the drawer overlay to close it.

## Severity
Low

## Priority
Low

## Status
Open

## Recommendation
Attach a `keydown` event listener to the window in the `CopilotDrawer.tsx` component that sets `isOpen(false)` when `e.key === 'Escape'`.
